# Game Rules

## 1. Goal
The objective is to find the shortest path (the fewest number of moves) for a specific mole to reach a designated target space on the board.

## 2. Components
* **The Board:** A grid consisting of squares, some of which are separated by walls.
* **The Moles:** Four moles of different colors and a neutral black mole.

## 3. Setup
* One device, preferably with a larger screen, will be used as the host of the game. This is where the main action will happen.
* Players will join the game from their own devices and use them for bidding and proving their claims.

## 4. Gameplay Flow
### The Reveal
At the beginning of each round a random square on the board lights up in one of the moles' colors.
* **Target:** The mole of that specific color must reach the target on the board.
* **Special Case (Multicolor Target):** If a "universal" target is revealed, **any** of the moles can be used to reach that target.

### The Mental Search
Once the target is revealed, everyone simultaneously searches for a path from the mole's current position to the target. And the clock is ticking!

### Making a Bid
1.  When a player finds a path, they enter the number of moves.
2.  Until the time runs out players can change their answers as they wish.
3.  The live ranking of bids can be seen on the host screen.

### Demonstration
Once the timer runs out:
1.  The player who bid the **lowest number** demonstrates their solution.
2.  If multiple people bid the same lowest number, the person who claimed it first goes first.
3.  The player sequentially gives the moves to prove their count.
4.  **Success:** If the solution is valid and matches their bid (or is lower), they win the round and get a point.
5.  **Failure:** If the solution is invalid or requires more moves than they bid, the player with the next lowest bid tries to demonstrate.

## 5. Movement Rules
Moles in this game have very limited movement:
* **Straight Lines:** Moles move horizontally or vertically.
* **No Brakes:** Once a mole starts moving, it **must** continue in that direction until it hits an obstacle.
* **Obstacles:**
    1.  **Walls:** The edges of the board or the walls separating the squares.
    2.  **Other Moles:** A mole acts as a solid wall for any other mole.
* **A "Move":** A move is defined as one mole sliding in one direction until it hits an obstacle. Changing direction or moving a different mole counts as a new move.
* **Auxiliary Moles:** You can move other moles (not just the target mole) to use them as "stoppers" to create new paths for the target mole. Every move by these helper robots counts toward your total bid. This is the sole purpose of the neutral black mole's existence.

## 6. Winning the Game
The game ends after a pre-established number of rounds. The player with the most points wins.
